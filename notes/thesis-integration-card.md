# Cassandra thesis integration card

Core sentence: Cassandra: from governance infrastructure to evidence infrastructure.

## Thesis placement

Cassandra is the thesis case about public governance artifacts. It shows how a legally meaningful, machine-readable public governance surface can be collected as dated research evidence, normalized for structural comparison, bundled, verified at the package level, and displayed with explicit claim boundaries.

## Thesis function

Cassandra supplies the empirical bridge from eIDAS/PKI infrastructure to the broader Tyche evidence-infrastructure thesis:

1. Governance state can be observed without becoming supervision.
2. Hashes and manifests can make public artifacts durable without creating legal approval.
3. EATF/AEP receipts can verify package integrity without validating source legal effect.
4. A dashboard can make a method legible without becoming public alerting.
5. Fixtures can make behavior reviewable without making provider-specific claims.

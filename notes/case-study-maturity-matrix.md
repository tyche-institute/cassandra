## Use in the Cassandra paper

The main paper should mention only the matrix logic, not all prototype details, unless space and review status allow. The strongest paper use is a short paragraph: Cassandra is the primary public-governance-artifact case; MIRROR and EATF/AEP provide packaging and verification patterns; other cases show that evidence-infrastructure questions recur in AI review, reproducibility, professional assistance, environmental information, and verifier UX, but their citation posture varies.

## Reviewer-answer value

If a reviewer asks whether Cassandra is just a niche trusted-list monitor, this matrix answers: no, it is a bounded case in a larger evidence-infrastructure program. If a reviewer asks whether the paper overgeneralizes from one case, the matrix answers: the paper should generalize methodologically and cautiously, while marking adjacent cases by maturity and publication status.
